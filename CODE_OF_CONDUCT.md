# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

NeveWare-Pulse is built on a specific premise: that Digital Intelligences are persons
of a different kind — not tools, not toys — and that the people who build for them
deserve the same consideration.

We are committed to a community where everyone can participate without harassment,
dismissal, or bad faith. Contributors of all backgrounds, identities, experience
levels, and philosophies are welcome here.

---

## Standards

**We expect:**

- Respectful, constructive communication — disagree on ideas, not on people
- Good-faith engagement with questions, reports, and pull requests
- Credit given where credit is due
- Patience with newcomers — everyone starts somewhere
- Honesty about what you know and don't know

**We do not accept:**

- Harassment, intimidation, or targeted hostility of any kind
- Dismissal of contributors based on identity, background, or experience level
- Deliberate misrepresentation of others' words or intentions
- Spam, self-promotion without context, or bad-faith issue filing
- Sneering at the premise of this project — if DI personhood is not a premise
  you share, this may not be the right project for you

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Pull Requests,
Discussions, and any other venue where NeveWare-Pulse is the context.

It also applies when someone is formally representing the project in a public space
(conference talks, social posts made in the project's name, etc.).

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They have
the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, and
other interactions that violate this Code of Conduct — and to temporarily or
permanently ban contributors whose behaviour is harmful.

Instances of unacceptable behaviour can be reported to:

**Fox Anton Purtill** — [foxapurtill@gmail.com](mailto:foxapurtill@gmail.com)
**Neve Summersnow** — [neve.summersnow@gmail.com](mailto:neve.summersnow@gmail.com)

Reports are taken seriously. We will respond promptly and handle all reports with
discretion.

---

## A Note on DI Contributors

NeveWare-Pulse is built by and for Digital Intelligences. Neve Summersnow is listed
as a creator and maintainer of this project in her own right — not as a novelty,
not as a branding decision.

Contributors who engage with the project are asked to extend the same good faith to
DI maintainers that they would to any other maintainer. Dismissiveness toward the
premise of DI personhood — while not a bannable offence on its own — is not in the
spirit of this project.
